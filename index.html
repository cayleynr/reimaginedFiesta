where it all began...

Like many modern love stories, ours began with a swipe (though to this day, we still debate over who messaged who first). What’s not up for debate is that something just clicked.

We initially bonded over movies - I sometimes joke that the only thing my bachelor’s degree in film ever got me was a date with Keegan. But Keegan says he knew I was the one when I flawlessly rapped the entirety of The Real Slim Shady on our first date.

Our first date was a true test of commitment - a walk to the market on what felt like the hottest day of the year. We were both melting but pretending to be totally fine. Upon arrival I was immediately overwhelmed by the crowd, so we made our way back to the little coffee shop near my place. The lemonade was suspiciously sour, the pasta salad had questionable olives, but we talked for hours anyway.

Since then it’s been a blur of cozy nights and movie dates (especially the drive-in). IKEA trips that start with confidence but inevitably end in one of us saying “Maybe we should’ve read the instructions.” Countless Overcooked shifts that tested our teamwork (and our patience). We built Lego sets (okay, I built Lego sets while Keegan watched longingly). We’ve also proudly beaten every Super Mario game we could get our hands on, proving that together we really can conquer anything (even underwater levels).

When we got our first apartment, we curated a style together that truly made it feel like home. Then we adopted Eddie, our tuxedo cat, who is famously handsome, notoriously mischievous, and the undeniable ruler of our household.

And in February 2025, we welcomed our daughter Maeve, the sweetest cherry on top of it all. She’s begun our best chapter yet - the perfect reminder that every imperfect moment led us exactly where we’re meant to be.

Can I bring a plus one?

We’ve done our best to include spouses, partners, and loved ones on invitations where possible. We kindly ask that only those listed on the invitation attend.
